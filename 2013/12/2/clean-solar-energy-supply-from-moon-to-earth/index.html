CLEAN SOLAR ENERGY SUPPLY FROM MOON TO EARTH

by bensonshaji . 02 Dec 2013    
Tweet

Luna ring is an upcoming Japanese technology which supplies unlimited solar power from moon to earth by laying solar panel belt on the moon's equator. This would generate 13,000 terawatts of energy per year, this quantity is quite higher when compared to the United States power production in 2011 - which is 4,500 terawatts. The solar panel belt will be laid approximately 11,000 KM. Construction of this belt will be mined by robots and using natural resources from moon. And this construction will begin as soon as 2035.
