AMAZON SAYS DRONE DELIVERIES ARE THE FUTURE

by bensonshaji . 02 Dec 2013

Drone deliveries are the future says Amazon CEO Jeff Bezos, drones would be ready to take flight in four to five years.

"I know this looks like science fiction. It's not, says Bezos in an interview. Drones will drop your package and you pick it up, this way we can do half hour deliveries.

Bezos also said the drone model AMZN, Fortune 500 is currently being tested that can handle products under five pounds within a range of 10 miles.
