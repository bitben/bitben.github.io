DOGS CAN EVEN PREDICT HUMANS

by bensonshaji . 17 Dec 2013

Its amazing to know that dogs ability to understand humans is ever growing and they can even predict human behaviour. A study conducted by a group of scientist at the University of Abertay, Dundee shows that dogs ability to understand humans is innate and independent of training. Observations shows how dogs respond to visual clues from owners, as they gaze up at us, our pet dogs often seem to know what we are thinking. Their position as man's best friend is getting stronger all the time - because their ability to understand and predict human behaviour is actually growing with each generation, according to animal psychologists. Scientist have observed how 24 dogs, ranging from the higly trained to the totally untrained, reacted to people's unspoken commands, such as pointing at a location for the dog to move to. It is a result of breeding and genetic selection, so it will only get more advanced in the future.
