WOMENS SAFETY APP GUARDIAN LAUNCHED

by bensonshaji . 26 Dec 2013    
Tweet

Guardian is an ultimate security app developed to track and ensure the safety of your dear ones and especially women, after the tragic incident of nirbhaya. Microsoft india today launched its new windows phone app that can track your buddies by sending repeated sms when they change their location. This app has the capability to send SMS, Emails and Facebook private group posting, notifying them of the emergency along with the location details. This app can be really helpful to ensure the safety of your dear ones. So far this app is available only for windows phone users but we can hope apps for alternate platforms like Android and iOS.
