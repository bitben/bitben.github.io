The tangible media group at MIT's Media Lab has unveiled a futuristic display made of atoms and not pixels.

You might have watched the movie Man Of Steel where you can see a new kind of technology where the display are actually made of solid objects and not flat screens. Well this is similar to it but for real. These displays are actually made of atoms that you can reach out and touch.
