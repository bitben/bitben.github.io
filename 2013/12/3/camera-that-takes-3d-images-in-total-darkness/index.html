CAMERA THAT TAKES 3D IMAGES IN TOTAL DARKNESS

by bensonshaji . 03 Dec 2013

Scientist have developed a camera that can take 3D images in almost total darkness using existing lasers and photon detecting technologies.

The system uses Lidar technique where lasers are methodically fired at an object in a grid pattern and calculates the time taken to reflect the light back to a sensor which slowly build a 3D model of its surroundings.
