EGYPTIAN-ROCK-ART-DATES-BACK-4000-YEARS-BC

by bensonshaji . 28 Dec 2013    
Tweet

An ancient piece of spider rock art has been found in the Kharga Oasis in Egypt which dates back to 4000 B.C . Ikram, a professor at the American University in Cairo noted that the rock art was possibly drawn in 4,000 B.C or earlier, before the unification of Egypt. The panel appears to depict a number of arachnids, and also features a star, which may be a symbolic illustration of spider's web. The researchers said the drawings are noteworthy if they are indeed spiders, as these would be unique depictions of such creatures in the Old World rock art.
