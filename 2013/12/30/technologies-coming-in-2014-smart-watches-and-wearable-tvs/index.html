TECHNOLOGIES COMING IN 2014 SMART WATCHES AND WEARABLE TVS

by bensonshaji . 30 Dec 2013

When it comes to technlogy 2013 was just a begining for something greater such as Smart Watches and Wearable. We can expect more to come in 2014 with advanced technologies than that of 2013.

Obviously you cannot expect a humanoid robot from Google to help you cook food and and keep your house clean, We might have to wait for another 2 decades for such things to happen. Instead you can expect to wake up with a wake up alarm of your wrist watch rather than your phone on a night table. Dont worry about your phone, it still looks the same as it is now, execpt that its size would be bigger and thinner.

You must have already come across Google glass Technology which is also an example for a Wearable Tech similar to Smart Watches. So far we must be knowing Google glass can be used as a Navigator, Camera, Message Notifier etc... What if we can expect a TV or Video Streaming on Wearable Glass for Entertainment? That will be a whole new level. It will actually be the smallest TV with bigger view.

Another Technology we came across was 3D Printing and Printers. We could possibly see 3D printers at home for commercial purpose rather than using it as a prototype. 3D printers can be really usefull to create needfull stuffs that is not available in the market today. For example a stylish laptop covering case for your new macbook.
