TSUNAMI HISTORY RECORDS DISCOVERED IN INDONESIA

by bensonshaji . 19 Dec 2013    
Tweet

An Indonesian cave of Sumatra is providing stunning record of Indian Ocean Tsunamis that took place thousands of years ago. A cave on the northwestern coast of Sumatra holds a remarkable record of big tsunamis in the Indian Ocean, The cave retains the deposits washed ashore by huge waves over thousands of years ago. Scientist are using the site to help determine the frequency of catastrophes like the event of 26 December 2004. Scientist say that repeated tsunami deposits can be found in this cave from about 7500 to 3500 years ago.
