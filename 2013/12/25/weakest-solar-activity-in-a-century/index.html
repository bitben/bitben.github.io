WEAKEST SOLAR ACTIVITY IN A CENTURY

by bensonshaji . 26 Dec 2013    
Tweet

scientist from San Francisco says that sun's current space cycle is the most weakest activity in a century. Our star is now at "solar maximum" the peak phase of its 11 year activity cycle. But this solar maximum is weak, and the overall current cycle known as solar cycle 24, conjures up comparisions to the famously feeble solar cycle 14 in the early 1900s, researchers said. None of us alive have ever seen such a weak cycle. So we will learn something this time says scientist.
