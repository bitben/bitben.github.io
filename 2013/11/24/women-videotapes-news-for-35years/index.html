WOMEN VIDEOTAPES THE NEWS FOR 35 YEARS NON-STOP

by bensonshaji . 24 Nov 2013    
Tweet

Marion strokes is the lady who made this legacy by recording the news begining from 1977 to 2012 has an incredible 140,000 tapes of local, national and cable news. She had to feed 6hr tape, 4times a day into the video recorder for about 35 years.

Stokes was a former librarian who started recording news as a hobby but then she became obsessed to do it on a regular basis. Infact she spent most of her life time paying attension to news and recording them to video tapes. She would wake up early in the morning to change the video tapes for continual recording and would ask her family members to do the same if she wasn't home. She would cut her short meals at restaurants to rush home before tapes ended. And when she got too old to keep up, she trained a younger helper named frank to run the various recording equipment.
