AN ISLAND NATION IN THE PACIFIC OCEAN IS ABOUT TO DROWN BY CLIMATE CHANGE

by bensonshaji . 25 Nov 2013

Soon an entire country will be wiped of the map because of rise in sea level caused by global warming. Kiribati is the island in the pacific ocean, that will start to disappear around 2030 according to an article at bloomberg business. The president of this island Anote Tong is arguing that the industrialized nations of the world are murdering his country. If scientist are correct, the ocean will swallow most of Kiribati island before the end of this century, and perhaps much sooner than that. As a result 103,000 kiribati citizens would become refugees in other countries. The strange thing is people will become refugees as a consquense of gloabal warming and not war or famine.
