WORLDS FIRST GREEN HELICOPTER

by bensonshaji . 25 Nov 2013    
Tweet

Volocopter is a revolution in aviation made in Germany by the company E-volo. It is simpler, safer and cleaner than normal helicopters. The Volocopter is environment friendly and emission-free private helicopter. Instead of one combustion engine, eighteen electrically driven rotors propel it.

The maiden flight and first test flights were conducted in the dm-arena in Karisruhe with the prototype of the 2 person VC200. Based on this model, it will be prepared for series production in the coming years. There are already numerous requests for the volocopter from all around the world said Alexander Zosel, Managing director of e-volo.
