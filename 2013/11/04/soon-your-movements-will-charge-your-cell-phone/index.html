SOON YOUR MOVEMENTS WILL HELP YOU CHARGE YOUR CELL PHONE

by bensonshaji . 04 Nov 2013

The mobile generator company Genneo has made a revolutionary change in the field of electronics by developing a new device that turns your motion into power for electronic devices such as mobiles, cameras, tablets and other USB charged electronics. Basically Kickstarter is a device that has an on-board battery which stores the charge based on natural motions of human body, This device will be provided with a USB port where you can plug your USB charger to recharge your cell phone. What if you got to charge your cell phone immediately without waiting for it to get charged through natural motions? Well, you can shake this device hard enough so that charge rate would be 20 times greater. They can also be pre-charged like auxiliary batteries with the added advantage of keeping themselves topped-off as they are carried.

Even if this device comes handy in our daily life, it would be much more convenient if the same technology can be implemented in the cell phone batteries.
