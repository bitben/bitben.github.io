SOON ANDROID WILL BE ON COMPUTERS

by bensonshaji . 26 Nov 2013

Intel is working on a version of android very hard so that you will be able use it instead of Windows in your corporate notebook. At an investor meeting last week, Intel was discussing to scale down Android to 64-bit so it could run on Intel's entire line of notebook processors, from the humble Atom all the way up to high-end Core processors. By now if you are thinking about the price on such a device, its certainly cheaper as low as $200, because Microsoft charges for windows where as Google gives Android free of charge. Lets hope that Intel's dreams are much bigger than consumer-oriented Android notebooks.
