OWN A PERSONAL JETPACK NEXT YEAR

by bensonshaji . 20 Sep 2013

Everyone must have had a dream about flying like the super heroes of the previous decades at least once in there lifetime, well that dream is not a dream anymore. Martin aircraft has announced to launch "Martin Jet pack" in the next year. This will be the most awaited tech and first of its kind for the military, civil defense and recreational.

Some features of Martin Jet pack?

Max traveling speed - 74km/h >> Range - 30km >> Fuel capacity - 45ltr

How much does it cost?

The exact price of the jet pack has not been set yet since the project is still in the development stage, However Martin Aircraft Company is targeting a sales price of US$100,000 for the recreational version of the aircraft through dedicated tourism ventures, pilot training and private recreational sales.

As you can see that the price is not cheap and affordable, the best way to experience this jet pack is through authorized dedicated tourism ventures.
