SOLAR PLANES AND GOOGLE

by bensonshaji . 21 Sep 2013    
Tweet

Days are not too far to see solar planes flying around, Solar Impulse is the first of it's kind to fly day and night across America without any fuel. Google became an Official Internet Technology Partner to promote its goal of circumnavigating the globe in 2015 using only solar energy. It is not a wonder to see these companies co-operate with each other who tend to share logical mindsets like pioneering spirit, innovation, engineering excellence and clean technologies to protect natural resources. The Solar Impulse Aircraft is able to fly throughout the day and night without burning any fuel. The plane is powered entirely by solar panels and on-board batteries, which charge during the day and enable the plane to fly in the night when the sun goes down. The carbon-fiber plane weighs about the same as a small car, and generates roughly the same power as a small scooter, company officials have said.
