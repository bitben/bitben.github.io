Google's android OS is making its way to mate with Cars from Phone. Audi is making an imminent shift to Android from its Audi MMI Operating system to power its multimedia platforms. Consumers Electronic Show (CES) in Las Vegas has been kickstarted today and we can expect exciting car-tech announcements of Audi, Honda and GM on their shift to Android for in-car systems.

Could this be a sign or stepping stone for "SMART CARS"? We already know that self driving cars are already on its way and Google is making excessive use of it to gather data for Google Maps. Introducing Android OS for cars is just for Multimedia Purpose or can we expect more in the Future?
