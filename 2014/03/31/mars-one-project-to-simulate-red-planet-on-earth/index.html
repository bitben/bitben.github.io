MARS ONE PROJECT TO SIMULATE RED PLANET ON EARTH

by bensonshaji . 01 Apr 2014

As we know that mars one mission has already picked future crew members for one way trip to the red planet, now they are planning to simulate red planet on earth to prepare them for their life on Mars.

The non profit team hasn't picked a location for the first simulator but they are eager to get started constructing actual hardware for this mission. Bas Lansdorp, co-founder of Mars One, made a statement saying "We are going from theory to practice" shows that the team is ready to begin the simulator project in earth.
