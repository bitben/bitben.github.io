CHANCE TO WIN 10000 DOLLARS IF YOU ARE A HACKER

by bensonshaji . 14 Jul 2014    
Tweet

Tesla Motors has announced at the SyScan Conference in Beijing that white hat hackers will have the chance to win $10000 if they can successfully hack into a Model S and manipulate the cars controls remotely

Tesla is trying to convince its consumers that gasless(electric) vehicles are the future of driving, and this effort will find success only if they can prove that their software systems are safe from hackers. In order to test their software security they are inviting white hat geeks to participate in SyScan Conference and make an attempt to hack into their software systems to control the car remotely.
