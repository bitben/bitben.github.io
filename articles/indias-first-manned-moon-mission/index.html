India's first manned moon mission

posted on: 01-01-2014 23:56 PM / written by: bensonshaji / For: science, moon, india

Popularity

Tweet

India is getting set to follow the foot steps of Neil Armstrong through its first manned moon mission. The Indian Air Force has been given the responsibilities for this mission says Air Marshal DP Joshi. Memorandum of Understanding has been signed between ISRO and the defence ministry. The Indian Air Force is setting up a team for the mission. Rupees 10 Crore has been set aside for the study. The IAF feels fighter pilots are best suited for the moon mission.
