Mars one mission - one way ticket to mars

posted on: 02-11-2013 07:39 AM / written by: bensonshaji / For: science, mars, universe, mars-one

Popularity

Tweet

Over the years humans have explored universe to a billion light years from earth, and now it is time to start settling in the universe. Mars will be the stepping stone and Mars-One is a mission to establish human settlement on mars by 2023. Did I mention that this is a one way trip? That means there is no coming back. You will be travelling 54 to 400(min and max) million kilometer(depending on the distance of earth and mars based on orbit variation) from earth for about 7 months in space. Do you think that your travel is worth landing in mars? This ambitious mission of Mars-One is to establish a human colony in a planet where atmosphere is hardly adjustable to mankind and to travel millions of kilometers in space where the safety of the people in the cargo cannot be guaranteed.
