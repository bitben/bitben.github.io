China shows of moon rover model

posted on: 06-11-2013 10:31 AM / written by: bensonshaji / For: technology, moon, china

Popularity

Tweet

When everyone is busy with mars mission, China is preparing itself to send a Moon rover that will explore moon’s surface in an upcoming mission. China has offered a rare glimpse of its rover from secretive space programme on tuesday. China has ambitious space goals, including to send its first probe to land on the moon by the end of this year.

The gold coloured rover model looks cute like animation character Wall-E. The rover’s designer, Shanghai Aerospace System Engineering Research Institute, said the real thing would be lifted a lot by long March 3B rocket scheduled to be launched in early December.

This lunar rover has some features like, Autonomous Navigation, Wheels are able to grip the powdery surface of the moon and can travel up to 200 metres per hour with inclination up to 30 degrees.
